Fun Searcher

Never Miss An Exciting Moment Again

Search for events with customized inputs (event name, type and city) and show them on a map! Click on those pins to get more details.
You can also click on the map to get events near where you just clicked (within 200 miles) filtered by your inputs.

By default, this app searches for events in the US, and pagination is implemented when your search result has more than 20 data.

This app is built with TicketMaster and Google Map API for OOP JavaScript demostration.
